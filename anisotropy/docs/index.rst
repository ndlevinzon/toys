Anisotropy — cryo-EM protein orientation at the air–water interface
=====================================================================

Educational pipeline for **solvent-accessible surface (SAS) meshing**, **patch-wise
interfacial chemistry**, and **Boltzmann orientation sampling** against a hybrid
lattice-gas / electrostatic Hamiltonian at a vitrified water slab.
